Orange County Hidden Gems

Get a local's insights into the hidden gems of Orange County

Fullerton Arboretum

The Fullerton Arboretum is a 26 acre botanical garden located on the campus of Cal State Fullteron. It is not as large as the Costa Mesa Arboretum, but provides long trails and a great place to walk and enjoy nature, unique plants, and sculptures.

Crescent Bay Point Park

Crescent Bay Point Park is a small park on a hill overlooking Laguna Beach. This is a local favorite to watch sunsets and have a nice picnic. If you're looking for something more relaxed and slightly off the beaten path, this provides.

The Bowers Museum

Located in Santa Ana, the Bowers museum houses several exhibitions at any given time, guaranteeing that there is something of interest to every visitor. These exhibitions range from ancient Chinese art to modern and contemporary pieces.
