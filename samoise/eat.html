Eat your way through Orange County

Orange County is home to eateries and restaurants of any type of food you can imagine. From casual beach side cafes to dining along the Newport Harbor, explore what Orange County has to offer.

Ruby's Diner, Huntington Beach

Ruby's Diner is a chain with dozens of locations around the country. However, none are as iconic as the location on the end of the Huntington Beach Pier. After a day at the beach, patrons can enjoy a nice, sit-down meal, or order some fries and a shake at the convinient window. This Ruby's is iconic and a must visit during a trip.

Cauldron Creamery, Costa Mesa

Cauldron is a local delicacy that was started in a garage in Orange County in 2014. The ice cream is made in front of you using dry ice, and served in a one of a kind Puffle Cone. When looking for a cold treat to cool down after a long day, Cauldron is a great option.

Mi Casa, Orange

Mi Casa is best described as a hole in the wall taco shack in Orange. If you're looking for a fancy 5 star restaurant, this isn't the place. But if you are looking for some of the best tacos in Southern California, look no further.
