Discover what's happening in the OC.

Pageant at the Masters

Every year, towards the end of the summer, Laguna Beach hosts the Pageant at the Masters. Laguna Beach is well known for its vibrant art scene. And this well renowned exhibition of art is another example of the city's investment in the arts.

Orange County Fair

The OC Fair is held every summer during July and August, and attracts millions of visitors. People come to eat fair food, see unique attractions, play fun games, and ride some rides.

Surfing Santa Competition

The surfing Santa Competition, held every year in late November, involves participants dressing as Santa Claus and attempting to win a surfing competition.
