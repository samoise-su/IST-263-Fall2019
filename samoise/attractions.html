Attractions

Orange County attracts millions of visitors every year. Discover why.

Disneyland

Disneyland and California Adventure attract millions of visitors each year. Located in the heart of Orange County in Anaheim, Disneyland is known for its family friendly atmosphere and providing a magical experience to children and adults alike.

Catalina Island

Catalina Island is a small island about an hour's boat ride off the coast. Most days it is visible from the shore in Newport Beach and thousands of visitors take the ferry or a helicopter to visit every year. On the island there are several restaurants and beaches to relax at, as well as a historic theater and a tour around the island.

The Bowers Museum

Located in Santa Ana, the Bowers museum is one of the most popular art exhibitions in the state. With several exhibitions at any given time, there is something of interest to every visitor. These exhibitions range from ancient Chinese art to modern and contemporary pieces.

Whale Watching

A popular option for many visitors is a whale watching expidition. There are several local businesses that operate tours of the coastline and a chance to see one of these majestic animals in person.
