Beaches

The golden beaches that stretch along the Orange County coastline are a national treasure and are not to be missed when visting.

Laguna Beach

Laguna Beach is famous for picturesque views and mansions right off the cliffs. Tv shows such as the Hills have been filmed in Laguna and it is a favorite spot to relax for many celebrities. In Laguna Beach, you never know who you'll run into.

Huntington Beach

Huntington Beach is also known as Surf City, USA. Home of the US Open of Surfing (which attracts millions of viewers every August), Surf City is also a great spot to walk along the boardwalk or visit any of dozens of surf shops.

Newport Beach

Newport Beach is known for extravagant houses and yaughts just as big as the house they're attached to. For those of us who don't live in these mansions, it is popular to rent a stand-up paddle board and paddle through the bay. Newport Beach also has its fair share of famous surf spots. The Wedge is known as one of the largest and most dangerous waves in the world, and attracts thrill seekers from all over.
